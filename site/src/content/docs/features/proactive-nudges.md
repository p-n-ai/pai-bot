P&AI Bot doesn't wait for students to come back — it reaches out when it's time to review.

## How It Works

A scheduler runs every 5 minutes checking for:
- Topics with due reviews (based on SM-2 intervals)
- Pending goals approaching their deadlines
- Streak milestones worth celebrating

When a review is due, the bot sends a personalized nudge message to the student on their chat channel.

## Quiet Hours

Nudges respect **quiet hours: 9:00 PM – 7:00 AM** (Malaysia Time). No student gets woken up by a math review reminder.

## Daily Cap

Maximum **3 nudges per day** per student to prevent notification fatigue. The scheduler tracks how many nudges each student has received today and stops when the cap is hit.

## Personalization

When AI nudges are enabled (`LEARN_AI_PERSONALIZED_NUDGES_ENABLED=true`), nudge messages include personalized context:

- Current streak count
- Active goal progress
- Specific struggle areas
- XP needed for next milestone

The nudge content is generated by the AI using the cheapest available model to keep costs low.

## Language Support

Nudges are sent in the student's preferred language (English, Bahasa Melayu, or 中文), following the same language preference stored from onboarding or the `/language` command.
